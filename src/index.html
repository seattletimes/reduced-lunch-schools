Students qualifying for free and reduced-cost lunch by school district

The WIAA is exploring a socioeconomic solution to the widening competition gap. For every percentage point above the state average (43%) of students qualifying for free or reduced-cost lunch, the school's enrollment figure, that's used to determine its WIAA classification, would be equally reduced.
